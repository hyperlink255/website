What is mood of the market?

The Market Mood Index (MMI) is a reflection of the collective mood of the market, whether it’s bullish or bearish. The Market Mood Index is a metric that tracks the overall sentiment of the stock market. Investors and traders who participate in the market calculate it based on their opinions and emotions.
